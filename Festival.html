Aharya Festival 2023

Four full days of blissful celebration

This time team Aharya is conducting Aharya festival 2023 in association with Bharathanatyam department of Kalady sree sankaracharaya university of Sanskrit. Festival starts on May 11th - 14th 2023. We have different venues inside the campus like university koothambalam, open auditorium, and few others

Famous artists across our country are joining us :

Madhurai R Muralidharan sir
Parshwanath Upadhaye sir
Dr. Shankar kandasamy sir

Shruthi Upadhye
Adithyan pv

These eminent artists have extended their hands to join us on these dates.
