HIGHLIGHTS OF 2024

The Grand Experience

An intensive four-day residential workshop focusing on Bharatanatyam and Mohiniyattam — the 2024 edition brought together some of the finest classical artistes and committed students from across the country.

Artistry on Stage

Breathtaking performances unveiled on a grand stage, where traditional classical dance met meticulous production values. A celebration of form, rhythm, and devotion to the art.

Masterclasses

Intensive sessions with gurus exploring the nuances of Abhinaya, Nritta, and classical choreography.

A Family of Artists

Dancers from across India gathered in Kalady, forging bonds and a shared love of classical heritage.

A Memorable Finale

The closing ceremony of 2024 left an indelible mark, setting the stage for an even grander 2025 edition.
