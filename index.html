Team Aharya

Centre for Performing Arts

Aharya School of Classical Dance is a premier institution dedicated to the teaching, preservation and promotion of Indian classical dance forms. Focused on creating a strong foundation for dancers, the school offers comprehensive training in Bharatanatyam, Kuchipudi, and Mohiniyattam, Expert instructors with years of experience and deep knowledge guide students, ensuring they receive world-class education and mentorship.
