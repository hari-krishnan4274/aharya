HIGHLIGHTS OF 2023

The Beginning: Day 1

Nritya sessions by Sri Madhurai R Muralidharan featuring Ayyappa Kavutuvam, followed by a masterful recital by Dr. Shankar Kandasamy.

Rhythm & Grace: Day 2

Intensive workshop covering Samskriti Vandana and Thillanas. Featuring Chithrapadaravanam – an artistic depiction of Seetha-Ravana.

Mastery in Motion: Day 3

Advanced Nattuvangam course by K.S Balakrishnan and a brilliant Bharathanatyam Margam performance by Dr. Janaki Rangarajan.

Grand Finale: Day 4

Chaari & Karanaas session, concluding with Punyah Dance Company’s "Abha" performance featuring Parshwanath Upadhye.
