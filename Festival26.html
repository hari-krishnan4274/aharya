About The Festival

Over the past three years, the Aharya National Dance Festival has become a highly anticipated four-day event, drawing participants from across India and beyond. The festival features workshops on various aspects of dance, including Abhinaya, warm-ups, Thaalam, Chaaris, and Karanas, among others offering a comprehensive learning experience for dancers at all levels. Additionally, the festival serves as a platform for emerging artists to showcase their talent. In addition to workshops, Aharya organizes specialized classes and performances led by senior artists, fostering an environment of artistic growth and professional development. Renowned performers such as Sankar Kandaswami, Madurai R. Muralidharan, Dr. Janaki Rangarajan, Parshwanath Upadhye, and Sreelakshmi Govardhan have graced the event, further elevating its prestige and impact.

The festival also features initiatives like the 'Mirror of Aharya,' which nurtures promising talent, and the 'Aharya Sabha,' a platform for artists and art enthusiasts in rural areas to showcase their talent and discuss various art forms, ensuring inclusivity and fostering a deeper connection with the community.
