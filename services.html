Our Services

The Gurukula Spirit

Aharya School of Classical Dance

Working in association with Team Aharya, Aharya School of Classical Dance functions as a dedicated centre for systematic training in Indian classical dance, rooted in tradition, discipline, and aesthetic excellence.

Our training follows the Gurukula spirit — nurturing artistry with depth, clarity, and cultural responsibility.

"We are not a competition-oriented training centre. Our focus is on cultivating true artistic understanding, classical integrity, and a lifelong relationship with the art form."

Faculty

Guided by an exceptional lineage of artistes, our faculty brings decades of rigorous classical training and performance experience to the classroom.

  • Praveen Kaimadathil
  • Sreelakshmi Warrier
  • Aparna Devi
  • Anjali Sankar
  • Adithya Sivadas

Levels of Training

Our training is centred on technique, abhinaya, theory, stage discipline, and a deep respect for the classical tradition.

  • Beginner Level
  • Intermediate Level
  • Advanced Level
  • Mothers’ Special Batch

Online Services

Embracing modern mediums without compromising on traditional rigor. All training modules offered offline are strictly available in online mode globally.

  • Online Nāṭyaśāstra Workshops
  • Bharatanatyam Item Workshops
  • Theory & Abhinaya Sessions
  • Structured Level-Based Online Training
