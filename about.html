Aharya Centre for Performing Arts

Team Aharya Centre for Performing Arts is a distinguished organization committed to the preservation, promotion, and growth of Indian classical dance and performing arts. Focussed on nurturing talent, fostering creativity, and advancing technical proﬁciency, the Centre serves as a vibrant hub where artists explore and deepen their expertise in traditional dance forms such as Bharatanatyam and Kuchipudi,

Through a variety of initiatives, including the prestigious Aharya National Dance Festival and Vachika Fest, Team Aharya creates valuable platforms for both emerging and established artists to showcase their skills and gain hands-on real-world experience, and interact with a broader artistic community.

The Centre’s commitment to excellence extends to offering high-quality workshops and professional-stage performances, enabling artists to reﬁne their techniques and enhance their stage presence. In addition, it provides exposure to the critical technical aspects of performance, such as lighting, sound, choreography, and theatre management. The comprehensive offerings ensure that artists are not only skilled performers but also well-rounded professionals in the competitive world of classical dance.

Driven by a mission to cultivate a deep appreciation for Indian classical arts, Team Aharya Centre for Performing Arts continues to expand its reach, attracting participants from across India and beyond. The centre fosters an environment of artistic excellence, cultural exchange, and professional development, making it a key player in the continued evolution of Indian classical dance.

Aharya School of Classical Dance

Aharya School of Classical Dance is a premier institution dedicated to the teaching, preservation and promotion of Indian classical dance forms. Focused on creating a strong foundation for dancers, the school offers comprehensive training in Bharatanatyam and Kuchipudi. Expert instructors with years of experience and deep knowledge guide students, ensuring they receive world-class education and mentorship.

At Aharya, students gain not only technical proﬁciency but also a profound understanding of the cultural and artistic signiﬁcance of these traditional dance forms. The school creates a nurturing environment that encourages artistic expression, discipline, and growth.

Aharya School of Classical Dance is committed to empowering the next generation of dance artists by equipping them with the skills, conﬁdence and artistic vision needed to excel in both the technical and creative aspects of classical dance. It is a place where tradition meets innovation, where passion for dance is nurtured into excellence and where aspiring dancers are inspired to reach their highest potential.

Visionary Leadership

Praveen Kaimadathil

Founder & CEO

Praveen Kaimadathil is a classical dance educator , choreographer and cultural organizer based in kalady, Kerala, India. He is the Founder of Team Aharya, an arts institution dedicated to nurturing Indian classical dance through structured training, performance platforms, and cultural initiatives. He holds a Bachelor’s and Master’s degree in Bharatanatyam from Sree Sankaracharya University of Sanskrit (SSUS) .
In 2018, he was awarded the prestigious CCRT (Centre for Cultural Resources and Training) Fellowship by the Government of India. Earlier, in 2017, he received the Vajra Jubilee Fellowship from the Kerala Department of Culture in recognition of his contribution to classical arts. As a visionary choreographer, he has conceptualised and directed several acclaimed dance productions including Chitrapadaravanam — a powerful narrative on Rama, Sita, Ravana and Hanuman; Krishna Shila — based on the history of the Guruvayur Temple; Maa — portraying the life of Adi Shankaracharya through the eyes of Aryamba; and Karnan — depicting the life and valor of Karna.
He is a dedicated exponent of classical Natya treatises such as the Natyashastra and Abhinaya Darpana, guiding the theoretical and aesthetic foundations of Indian classical dance. Under his leadership, Team Aharya organizes Kerala’s biggest residential classical dance workshop and National Dance Festival. classical dance. Through vision, discipline, and cultural commitment, Praveen Kaimadathil continues to contribute significantly to the growth and expansion of classical dance in India.
