About Us

TEAM AHARYA is an arts education organization established on 22nd September 2019,based in Kalady,Ernakulam. A group of young energetic artists are the nurturers of Aharya.When everyone turns to performances, Aharya took its first step to introduce Dance and Nritta - Natya Grandhas to the common people. Various programs are also organized as a part of this concept. This organization takes care to find people who are interested in art forms, economically backward areas and ensure good training in Dance and Music. Team Aharya is moving forward with many missions,conducting performances and presentations of major indian classical dances, Bharathanatyam, Mohiniyattam, Kuchippudi, Kathakali, Odissi, Kathak, Manipuri, Sattriya, and other traditional art forms with aiming to reach all over the world.
